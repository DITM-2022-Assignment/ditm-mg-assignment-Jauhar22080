How It Affects Us

One of the first ways we will be affected by trash is that our fish will start eating trash therefore causing our fish to be toxic and unhealthy for us to eat. This will then mean when we cook the fish we catch it could cause food poisoning and we will be more likely to get sick. Another problem with ocean trash is that our natural marine life will start to die off as trash starts to pollute the enviroment. One not often talked about reason ocean trash is bad is becuase the ocean actually produces appromixatly 50% of the air we breath so if we were to pollute the ocean it would affect the air we breathe causing it to be more toxic.
